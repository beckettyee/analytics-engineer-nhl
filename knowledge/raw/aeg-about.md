# We Are AEG

![A video will be played](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/W55WEZw3mxw/sddefault.jpg)

##### Creating unforgettable memories is what we do.

What do you do for an encore after entertaining over 100 million guests each year? As the world's leading sports and live entertainment company, we continually turn it up a notch with venues, sports franchises, music festivals, entertainment districts, ticketing platforms, global sponsorships, and innovative experiences that keep fans coming back for more year after year. Twenty years and counting... and we're just getting started! We set high standards and are committed to driving meaningful change in the world through philanthropy, sustainability and diversity, equity and inclusion.

We aspire to excite and delight fans across the world by delivering the most compelling sports and live entertainment events in the countries where we operate. We offer a depth and breadth of sports, music and live entertainment experiences that are unmatched around the world. Each of our divisions has a proven track record of producing groundbreaking events and experiences that give the world reason to cheer.
